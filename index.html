Visualisations of Meat Data

World Map of Meat Consumption

About Meat Consumption

This visualization shows the global meat consumption by country for the year 2021. Countries with higher meat consumption are represented with darker shades of red, while lower consumption is lighter. The map highlights trends across different continents and how meat consumption varies around the world.

Stacked Bar Chart of Meat Production

About Meat Production

This stacked bar chart provides an interactive look at the various types of meat production from 1961 onwards. It allows you to filter by meat types such as bovine, poultry, pigmeat, and more, providing insights into production trends over time.

Global Meat Production (1961 to 2021)

Global Meat Production

This area chart highlights the global production of meat over several decades, segmented by continents. It provides an insightful overview of how meat production has evolved across different regions since 1961.

GDP per Capita vs Meat Production per Capita (kg/person)

GDP vs Meat Production

The bubble chart compares the relationship between GDP per capita and meat production per capita. The size of the bubbles represents the GDP, while the position on the chart shows the correlation between wealth and meat production.
